French Toast Recipe

Description

French Toast has a variety of methods of how it can be made. Today, however, we will learn one of those methods. The dish we are making today is commonly made as a breakfast dish. A wonderful dish and easy to make, wheather you are in a rush or looking for a quick meal to whip up, this is the perfect dish that anybody can make.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 8 slices bread
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup, warmed
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine cinnamon and sugar and set aside briefly.
  2. In a 10-inch or 12-inch skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk together cinnamon mixture, eggs, milk, and vanilla and pour into a shallow container. Dip bread in egg mixture. Fry slices until golden brown, then flip to cook the other side.
  3. When ready, serve with syrup.
